Deep learning(DL) is a subset of ML which focus on a certain type of model, Neural Networks which are designed to mimic the functioning of the human brain. Research has proven that with massive datasets for training deep neural networks, they outperform almost all other types of ML models. However, despite having the best performance, it is difficult to interpret their working.

No manual feature selection is required in DL as the network performs the selection process internally without much hyperparameter tuning. Using Tensorflow and Keras, I have developed dozens of DL models in retail, finance and health which outshines supervised ML models.
