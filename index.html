Data Analytics

Examining your data to identify relevant patterns and trends is critical to ensure you meet customers' expectations as well as making informed decisions. Evidently, companies looking at what their data is saying and not doing it is creating a huge gap between poor performing businesses and top achievers across all industries. Analysing data is a process that starts from collection to cleaning and visualization, guided by different rules and principles. I find performing data analysis and visualization with technologies such as Pandas, Seaborn, Tableu and Excel enjoyable. I'm excited to help you understand your data by performing comprehensive data analysis.

Deep Learning

Deep learning(DL) is a subset of ML which focus on a certain type of model, Neural Networks which are designed to mimic the functioning of the human brain. Research has proven that with massive datasets for training deep neural networks, they outperform almost all other types of ML models. However, despite having the best performance, it is difficult to interpret their working. No manual feature selection is required in DL as the network performs the selection process internally without much hyperparameter tuning. Using Tensorflow and Keras, I have developed dozens of DL models in retail, finance and health which outshines supervised ML models.

Digital Transformation

If you are still using legacy systems in your business operations, you are missing out on a lot and risk being outperformed by the competition. Digitizing core business functions such as data collection, customer engagement, performance tracking, quality assurance and financial investing has proven to have huge returns. In addition, making use of cloud servers is tremendously cost-effective relative to setting up and maintaining own hardware. In light of this, allow me to be part of your crucial journey to cloud migration, whether opting to use AWS, Google Cloud Platform or Microsoft Azure cloud services.

Cloud Migration

Technology giants and other leading companies have set up massive data centers and server architecture globally, and avail them to other organizations for relatively low fees. I have the capacity and I'm excited about enabling companies to take full advantage of the most efficient cloud services by Amazon, Microsoft and Google.
