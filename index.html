Services

Project Planning and Management

I'm a Project Manager by profession having secured a Bachelor's degree in Project Planning and Management back in December 2019. In the contemporary business environment, companies have to undertake continous product modification and develop new ones as customer needs and preferences shift. Doing these requires first defining the objective at hand and the expected deliverables at the end of any business undertaking. In addition, the resource requirement and utilization have to be effectively strategized or a project may end in total failure.

Planning is crucial to determining the project timeline, budget, scope and implementation guidelines. Furthermore, it is paramount to effective matching of work-packages with the neccessary skills and talent. After accomplishing all these, monitoring and project tracking are inherent to ensuring the successful completion of a project within the given constraints of cost, quality, and schedule. I'm thrilled to be part of your project team, utilizing allocated resources to accomplish the desired deliverables.

Data Science
